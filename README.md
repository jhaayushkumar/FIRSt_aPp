3. Problem Statement
Long physical queues in hospitals, restaurants, government offices, salons, and other public places waste people’s time and cause frustration. Many customers are forced to wait physically for hours without any clear estimate of when their turn will come. This leads to inefficiency, customer dissatisfaction, and crowding, especially in busy cities.
.......................................................................................................................................
4. Proposed Solution / Idea
QueueLess will be a mobile application that replaces physical queues with a real-time virtual token system. Users can book their place in line remotely, track live queue status, and receive notifications before their turn arrives. Businesses can manage their queues digitally, improving efficiency and customer satisfaction.

.......................................................................................................................................
6. Key Features
Virtual token booking system for various services
Live queue status with estimated waiting time
Push notifications before the user’s turn
Location-based search for nearby service providers with active queues
Business dashboard to manage and monitor queues
Option to reschedule or transfer queue position to another person
